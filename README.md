# Bull Mail

Bull Mail is a smart platform that **identifies high-impact stock news daily** based on a user's watchlist and delivers it directly to their email. Stay ahead of the market without spending hours scanning news!

---

## 📌 Features

- Personalized stock watchlist tracking  
- Daily no noise high-impact news identification  
- Email delivery of curated stock news  
- Easy-to-understand summaries

## 🛠 How It Works

1. **User adds stocks** to their watchlist.  
2. **Bull Mail scans news** from multiple sources daily.  
3. **High-impact news is filtered** based on relevance and stock movement indicators.  
4. **Curated news is sent** directly to the user’s email inbox.
